Welcome to IFSDDA platform

Large-scale metabolites detection is a challenge in high resolution liquid chromatogram-tandem mass spectrometry (LC-MS/MS) for untargeted metabolomics. In this study, we develop an integrating full scan and data-dependent acquisition (IFSDDA) strategy for LC-MS/MS data acquisition, and improve performance of quantitative precision and metabolic features detection. IFSDDA improves the efficiency during data acquisition between MS and MS/MS scan through integrate datasets of full scan and DDA. IFSDDA have solved the problem of poor quantitative precision of DDA. IFSDDA substantially enhance metabolic coverage and quantitative precision for large-scale untargeted metabolomics.
